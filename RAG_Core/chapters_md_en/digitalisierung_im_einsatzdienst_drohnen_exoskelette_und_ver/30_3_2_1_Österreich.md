# 3.2.1 Österreich

- The fire brigade system in Austria is comparable to Germany. The fire brigade laws of the federal states oblige the municipalities to a security defense and the state fire brigade associations support the fire brigades in the training of their forces as well as the procurement of equipment.

- The fire brigade laws of the federal states oblige the municipalities to defend against danger and the state fire brigade associations support the fire brigades in the training of their forces as well as the procurement of equipment. Only the tasks of civil protection lie with the federal government.

- Only the tasks of civil protection lie with the federal government. According to the state laws, the municipalities are also entrusted with the responsibility for the establishment of a fire brigade and these prove to be responsible for the fire and danger protection.

- According to the state laws, the municipalities will also be responsible for setting up a fire brigade and these are responsible for fire and danger protection. Voluntary fire brigades form the personnel base.

- The voluntary fire brigades form the staff base. Only six cities in Austria have a professional fire brigade, including Vienna, Salzburg and Linz [18].
