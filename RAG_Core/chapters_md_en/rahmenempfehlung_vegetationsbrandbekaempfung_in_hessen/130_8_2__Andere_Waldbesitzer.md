# 8.2  Andere Waldbesitzer

- (31.12.2021) approximately 70 % of the Hessian forest are looked after by the Hessian forest company and its forest offices. In the case of forest fires, the local forestry specialist responsible for the management and care of the forest area is involved in the technical management of the forest according to § 6 para. 2 HWaldG.

- In the case of forest fires, the forest expert responsible for the management and care of the forest area in accordance with § 6 para. 2 HWaldG participates in the technical operation management. These can be, for example, the forest authorities or district managers of the state enterprise Hessen Forst, the national park management or in the forest outside of the national park Kellerwald-Edersee the forest owner or his expert representation.

- This may, for example, be the management of the forest office or the management of the district of Hessen Forst, the national park management or the forest outside the Kellerwald-Edersee National Park, which is not managed by Hessen Forst, or its competent representation. If, in the event of an emergency or in the event of a fire, there is no information about the owner of the forest or his expert representation or if, due to the short-term nature, these are not available, the local forestry office of the Hessen Forst regional office is to be used as the Lower Forestry Authority for advice in the field of operation.

- If there is no information about the owner of the forest or his expert representation in the event of an emergency or a fire or if these are not available due to the short-term nature of the forest, the local forestry office of the state enterprise Hessen Forst should be used as the Lower Forestry Authority for advice in the field of operation. Depending on local circumstances, it is important to carry out the necessary, regular training events and forest fire control exercises in cooperation with the forest owners not cared for by Hesse Forst – especially those with forest enterprises with their own staff.

- Depending on local circumstances, it is desirable to carry out the necessary regular training events and forest fire control exercises in cooperation with forest owners not supervised by Hessen Forst – especially those with forest enterprises with their own staff.
