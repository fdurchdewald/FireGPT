- Forest and vegetation fire fighting in Hesse Kassel, June 2023 III Foreword This framework recommendation was drawn up on behalf of the Hessische Landesfeuerwehrschule (HLFS) by a working group specifically set up for this purpose (AG).The working group consisted of the following members: Dr. Heiko Neumeier (HLFS) Carsten Hörner (HLFS) Dr. Thomas Kutschker (HMdIS) Heiko Schecker (LFV) Michael Plahusch (LFV) Carsten Lauer (LFV) Hendrik Frese (AGBF) Alexander Seiffen (AGBF) Jürgen Seitz (Werkfeuerwehrverband) Jörg Winter (Werkfeuerwehrverband) Dennis Schwalm (Hessen Forst) Henning Koch (Hessen Forst) By the Interior Ministry of the Free State of Thuringia as well as by the State Fire School Würzburg, the working group was provided with extensive material on the subject.
