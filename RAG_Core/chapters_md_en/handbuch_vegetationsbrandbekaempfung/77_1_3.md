- Forest fire hazard index (WBI) The actual prevailing risk situation in the individual Thuringian forest offices is determined on the basis of the forest areas, taking into account the measurement network of the German wet-service according to nature-spatial or climatic aspects by the DWD tages. Depending on the number of forecasting regions, different forest fire hazard levels (1-5) can apply within a forest office.
