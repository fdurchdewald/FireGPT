- Payment of costs for administrative assistance by other authorities The administrative assistance provided by the authorities is governed by the Basic Law of the Federal Republic of Germany, the Administrative Procedures Act of the Federal Government and the Thuringian Administrative Procedures Act.

- In this respect, it should be noted in principle that authorities agree to each other's assistance on request. §§ 4 et seq. Thuringian Administrative Procedures Act refers to the requirements and limits of mutual assistance.

- The conditions and limits of mutual assistance are set out in Sections 4 et seq. of Thuringia's Administrative Procedures Act. The costs incurred in the context of mutual assistance with the requested authority can be reimbursed on request by the requesting authority if you exceed EUR 25.00 in individual cases (no administrative costs, only costs actually incurred for materials or materials, etc.).

- The costs incurred as part of the mutual assistance with the requested authority may be reimbursed at the request of the requesting authority if you exceed EUR 25.00 in individual cases (no administrative costs, only costs actually incurred for materials or materials etc.). Costs relating to the mutual assistance provided by third parties commissioned by the requested authority must be reimbursed by the requesting authority.

- Costs related to mutual assistance provided by third parties appointed by the authority sought must be reimbursed by the requesting authority, for example, that, when requesting helicopters to combat fire, the actual costs incurred for helicopter operations must be borne by the requesting authority at the request of the requested authority.
