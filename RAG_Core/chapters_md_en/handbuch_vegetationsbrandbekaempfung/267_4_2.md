# 4.2

- Preliminary information In the field of vegetation fire control, the exploration and location determination has top priority in order to initiate appropriate measures and to work out the operation. The following possibilities for obtaining location information before arrival at the site of damage and danger are in principle available: • Information by alerting/deployment task • Information from senior executives • Information from management resources (alarm and deployment plan, developed in the context of deployment preparation) • Notifications from other authorities, experts or the population
