# 1.4

- Corridor fire prevention The prevention of fluorinated fires (mainly fields) can be implemented by simple measures taken by the responsible persons using the following examples: • Mowing field rain (strips of the field, before harvesting) • Rubbing or ploughing field margins: this prevents in the event of a fire of spreading on adjacent areas • Check machine exhaust systems for spark safety • Test machine dust protection systems for functionality • Check shafts and axes, for example, for wrapped straw to prevent hot runners • No welding/cutting and refuelling of harvesters on areas with combustible materials • Carry fire extinguishers to immediately initiate fire extinguishing measures • It is recommended to provide at least 3,000 litres of extinguishing water on site in case of extreme dryness (e.g. WBI/GLFI 4 or 5)
