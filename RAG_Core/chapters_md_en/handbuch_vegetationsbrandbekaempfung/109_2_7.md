# 2.7

- Training and further training At the district level Alarm and operational planning is also a fundamental task of the districts or district-free cities according to § 6 paragraph 1 no.3 ThürBKG. This applies in particular: • Set up alarm and operational plans • Continue alarm and operational plans • Alarm and operational plans that are in line with the alarm and operational plans of the municipalities as well as of the neighbouring districts and district-free cities, if necessary, coordinate Alarm and operational planning is carried out at the district level for special objects in the district or for the use of support point fire brigades and fire brigades with over-local tasks or as a framework alarm and operational plan for certain events, e.g. a forest fire.

- In particular: • Set up alarm and deployment plans • Rewrite alarm and deployment plans • Alarm and deployment plans that are in line with the alarm and deployment plans of the municipalities as well as the neighbouring districts and non-circular cities, if necessary, coordinate Alarm and deployment planning is carried out at the district level for special objects in the district or for the use of base fire brigades and fire brigades with over-local tasks or as a framework alarm and deployment plan for certain events, e.g. a forest fire. The same basic principles as for alarm and deployment plans of the municipalities are to be taken into account.
