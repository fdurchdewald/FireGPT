# 1. Allgemeine

- Information 10 11 Due to ThuringiaForest, the areas of the forest offices Weida, Jena-Holzland, Neustadt, Saalfeld-Rudolstadt, Bad Berka and Erfurt-Willrode were classified as areas with medium forest fire risk due to partly flat pine stocks and relatively dry soils.

- The remaining Thuringian forest offices are areas with a low risk of forest fire. Despite a low risk of forest fire, different factors (e.g. dead wood on the ground) pose a corresponding risk to ground fire.
