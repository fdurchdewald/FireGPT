# 8.3

- Operations management in the event of a disaster According to § 35 ThürBKG in conjunction with § 27 ThürBKG, the civil protection authority heads the civil protection operation.The civil protection staff is convened by the head of the staff as a political and overall responsible component and heads the operation.

- The Civil Protection Staff shall be convened by the Head of the Staff as a political and overall responsible component and shall direct the deployment. A corresponding management structure could be schematically broken down as follows: Determining and notification of the disaster event Communication without delay Communication as necessary Communication in an appropriate manner TLVwA TMIK neighbouring KatS public authority
