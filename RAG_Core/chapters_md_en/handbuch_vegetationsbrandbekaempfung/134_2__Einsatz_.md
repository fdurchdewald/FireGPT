# 2. Einsatz-

- Preparation 18 19 The 2-5 rule describes a rule of thumb for the conductability of tactical units. Core of the rule is that a leader can lead between two and five tactical units of the subordinate leadership level.

- The core rule is that a leader can lead between two and five tactical units of the subordinate leadership level. More than five tactical units must be distributed to several leaders/sub-leaders, who will then be led by the superior leadership.
