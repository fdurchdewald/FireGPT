# 9.2

- Costs for inter-regional operations The above statements on costs for operations to ward off local situations are generally also valid for operations to ward off inter-regional hazards (see Chapter 8.1). This means that the district as responsible for inter-regional fire protection and the inter-regional general assistance generally bears the costs of the operation, without harming the regulations for mutual assistance as well as regardless of who is responsible for the operation or has ordered the action.

- This means that the district, as the responsible body for super-local fire protection and general assistance, generally bears the costs of the operation, without harming the regulations for mutual assistance and regardless of who is responsible for the operation or has ordered the action. However, the costs for fire brigades with municipal equipment and other facilities of the municipalities of the hazardous area are excluded (§ 44 para. 1 and 2 no. 2 ThürBKG).

- However, the costs for fire brigades with community-owned equipment and other facilities of the communities of the hazardous area (§ 44 para. 1 and 2 no. 2 ThürBKG) are excluded.The regulation for mutual assistance of the districts as responsible for the supra-local fire protection and the supra-local general assistance applies, also in relation to the district-free cities (according to § 6 para. 3 ThürBKG).
