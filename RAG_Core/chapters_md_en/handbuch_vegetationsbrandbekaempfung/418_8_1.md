- The characteristic of super-locality is linked here to the existing danger situation. This makes clear § 6 para. 1 Nr. 4 ThürBKG, according to which the districts have to take the necessary measures, among other things, to effectively combat super-local hazards.

An extra-local danger exists if this as a contiguous danger or in the sum of individual hazards of different kinds captures or threatens to capture several municipalities and in which a common action (i.e. by the Landrat or its commissioner) is necessary (and therefore the overall operational management lies with this) for the defense.

- There is a super-local danger if this is a contiguous danger or the sum of individual hazards of different kinds, and if it is to cover several municipalities and if a common action (i.e. not only coordinated) is necessary for the defence (i.e. managed by the Landrat or its commissioner) (and thus the overall operational management is with him) is necessary.
