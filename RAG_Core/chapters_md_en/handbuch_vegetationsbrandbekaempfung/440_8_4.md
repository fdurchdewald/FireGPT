# 8.4

- Requesting materials from the KatS warehouses The Free State holds various means of use in a total of 4 decentralised civil protection camps • Nord in Nordhausen • Ost in Bad Köstritz • Süd in Hildburghausen • Mitte in Erfurt, which can also be used in the context of the vegetation fire fighting. These are e.g. extinguishing backpacks, tools for soil processing, forest fire backpacks, chain saws, gas-tight protective bridles, FFP3 masks, tents and much more.
