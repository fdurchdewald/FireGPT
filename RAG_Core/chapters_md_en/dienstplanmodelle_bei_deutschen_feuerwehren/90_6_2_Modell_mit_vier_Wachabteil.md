# 6.2 Modell mit vier Wachabteilungen

- The special feature of this model is that it is used for employees who wish to work within a 12-hour service while at the same time offering the service the additional shifts to be performed (marked V) in 24-hour service. The basic rhythm consists of two day shifts and two night shifts in 12-hour service with a subsequent leisure block of four days.
