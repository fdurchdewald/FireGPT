- 20 Entry into force I. Section Tasks and Responsibilities Art. 1 Task (1) Civil protection authorities have the task of averting disasters and taking the necessary preparatory measures (catastrophe protection).

- 1 Task (1) The task of the civil protection authorities is to prevent disasters and to take the necessary preparatory measures (catastrophe protection). (2) A disaster in the sense of this law is an occurrence in which the life or health of a large number of people or the natural basis of life or significant material values are at risk or damaged to an unusual extent and the danger can only be prevented or the disturbance can only be prevented and eliminated if the authorities, departments, organizations and the forces involved in civil protection work together under the direction of the civil protection authority.

- (2) A disaster in the sense of this law is an occurrence in which the life or health of a large number of people or the natural basis of life or significant material values are at risk or damaged to an unusual extent and the danger can only be prevented or the disturbance can only be prevented and eliminated if the authorities, departments, organizations and the forces involved in civil protection cooperate under the direction of the civil protection authority. (3) The statutory provisions applicable to those involved in civil protection shall remain unaffected, provided that this law does not contain any conflicting regulations.

- (3) The statutory provisions otherwise applicable to those involved in civil protection shall remain unaffected, provided that this law does not contain any conflicting provisions. Art.
