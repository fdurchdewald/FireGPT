# 2.1 Gesetzliche Grundlagen

- In the fire protection laws of the countries there are general requirements for the granting of expenses compensation by the task carriers6. Firefighters, who regularly provide fire service beyond the usual level, or are constantly approached for special services, can be adequately compensated.

- After that, firefighters who regularly provide fire service beyond the usual level, or who are constantly approached for special services, can be adequately compensated. These include the management and executives7 of the fire brigade, youth service center, equipment station, respirator service station, instructors as well as special functions and specialist advisors.
