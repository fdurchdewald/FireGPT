# 1.2.1 Abgrenzung

- The consideration is purely about expenses compensation within the scope of the service in the volunteer fire brigade, whose tasks are defined in the scope of the respective fire protection laws of the federal states2. Excluded from this were the regulations on wage or earnings loss as well as invoices of travel and travel costs.

- The regulations on the loss of wages and earnings as well as billing of travel and travel costs were excluded. The regulations of the Federal or Landesreisekostengesetz as well as the internal regulations and instructions of the managing authority are applied for missions of the fire brigade members.

- The regulations of the Federal or Landesreisekostengesetz as well as the internal regulations and instructions of the managing authority are applied for missions of the fire service members. Likewise, the expenses compensation for functionaries of the fire service associations, such as chairman, secretary, cashier, are not evaluated.

- Equally, the expenses compensation for officials of the fire brigade associations, such as chairman, secretary, cashier, are not evaluated. These are predominantly private law agreements under the association law.

- These are mostly private agreements under the law of association. Some of these regulations are also to be found in the federal states.

- In some cases, regulations of the federal states are also to be found, but these are not taken into account in the final evaluation of this technical work.
