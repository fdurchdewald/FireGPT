# 2.1 Literaturrecherche

- The method of literature research has been chosen to verify the existence of publicly available information in relation to the research questions and/or the work assignment of this specialist work. If available knowledge should then serve as a basis for answering the research questions and processing the work assignment.

- Where available, knowledge should then serve as a basis for answering research questions and working on the task. From the author's point of view, literature research is the only effective and efficient method to obtain possible publicly available information on a topic.
