# 3.3 Entwicklung eines Konzeptes zur Generierung von Einsatzerfahrung

- In order to develop the concept for generating operational experience for LFS fire brigade instructors, the survey of the LFS headmasters was first evaluated. From this evaluation, essential requirements for the concept were then defined.

- From this evaluation, essential requirements for the concept were defined. Based on these requirements and prior knowledge, a brainstorming took place to generate different concepts.

- Based on these requirements and their own prior knowledge, brainstorming took place to generate different concepts. Subsequently, the concepts were again checked for their compliance with the requirements and concepts were discarded which did not match the requirements.
