- § 7 Tasks of civil protection authorities (1) Civil protection authorities shall, at their discretion, take all preparatory measures in accordance with the laws in force to ensure effective civil protection, in particular the determination of the strength, structure, equipment and training of civil protection, unless otherwise provided by law, the formation of a civil protection management at the authority and the regulation of the Presidency, the establishment of civil protection plans and the coordination of the civil protection plans of the participating entities and bodies, the supervision of units and bodies, the organisation of exercises and training events, insofar as they are not carried out by the bodies of the units and institutions, the selection and training of management and management personnel, unless otherwise specified, the establishment, promotion and management of self-protection and education of the population.
