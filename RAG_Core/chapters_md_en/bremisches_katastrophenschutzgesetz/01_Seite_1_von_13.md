- 189) The Senate announces the following law adopted by the citizens' parliament: § 1 Task (1) Civil protection serves to protect the general public from dangers and damage caused by disasters.

- 189) The Senate announces the following law, adopted by the parliament (Landtag): § 1 Task (1) Civil protection is designed to protect the general public from the dangers and damage caused by disasters, including the preparation of disaster response and the fight against disasters.

- (2) Disaster within the meaning of this Act is an event which goes beyond the damage of daily life, life, health, the environment, significant physical assets or the vital supply of the population to such an extent that, in order to combat it, the competent authorities must cooperate with the units and facilities of civil protection and with the other persons drawn to the assistance under central management.

- (2) Disaster within the meaning of this law is an event which goes beyond the damage of daily life, life, health, the environment, significant material values or the vital supply of the population to such an extent that the competent authorities must cooperate with the units and facilities of civil protection and the other persons who are used to provide assistance under central management in order to combat it. § 2 Responsible persons (1) Civil protection is the responsibility of the country.

- § 2 Responsible persons (1) Civil protection is the responsibility of the country. (2) Insofar as the municipalities have to carry out tasks of civil protection, they act on behalf of the country.

- (2) Insofar as the municipalities have to perform civil protection tasks, they act on behalf of the country. (3) The municipalities are responsible for the implementation of civil protection.

- (3) The implementation of civil protection is the responsibility of the municipalities.
