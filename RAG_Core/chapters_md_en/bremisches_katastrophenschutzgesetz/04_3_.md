# 3.

- § 3 Civil Protection Authorities and their Responsibilities (1) The Senator for Home Affairs, Culture and Sports (Landesschalastigkeitsschutzbehörde) is responsible for civil protection in the state of Bremen.

- The technical competence of other national authorities remains unaffected. (2) The State Disaster Protection Authority coordinates civil protection at the country level.

- (2) The State Disaster Protection Authority coordinates civil protection at the national level. It supervises the municipalities (local disaster protection authorities).

- It oversees the municipalities (local disaster protection authorities). (3) The Senator for Home Affairs, Culture and Sport, the Mayor of Bremerhaven, is responsible for the implementation of civil protection in the municipalities (local disaster protection authorities) in the municipality of Bremen - with the exception of the city of Bremen's overseas port area in Bremerhaven.

- (3) The responsibility for the implementation of civil protection in the municipalities (local disaster protection authorities) lies in the municipality of Bremen - with the exception of the oversea port area in Bremerhaven - the Senator for Home Affairs, Culture and Sports, in the municipality of Bremerhaven - including the oversea port area in Bremerhaven - the mayor. (4) Unless otherwise provided, the local disaster protection authorities are responsible for the implementation of the law.

- (4) Unless otherwise specified, the local disaster protection authorities are responsible for the implementation of the law. § 4 Participation in civil protection (1) In civil protection, besides civil protection authorities, contribute to: technical and other institutions involved in the organisation of civil protection, public and private institutions with their units and institutions, natural and legal persons and associations of persons, which are used by the civil protection authority on the basis of an agreement or in accordance with §§ 12 and 13 for the provision of assistance in civil protection.

- § 4 Participation in civil protection (1) In civil protection, in addition to civil protection authorities, technical and other institutions involved in the organisation of civil protection, public and private bodies with their units and bodies, natural and legal persons and associations of persons used by the civil protection authority for the provision of civil protection assistance on the basis of an agreement or in accordance with §§ 12 and 13. (2) Contributing units and bodies within the meaning of paragraph 1 No. 2 are grouped summaries of persons and material under uniform management, according to technical tasks and to whose tasks the assistance in the event of disasters belongs.

- 2. The units and bodies referred to in paragraph 1 (2) shall be grouped together in order to provide assistance in the event of a disaster, in the form of a group of persons and materials under uniform management, with the task of providing assistance in the event of a disaster.
