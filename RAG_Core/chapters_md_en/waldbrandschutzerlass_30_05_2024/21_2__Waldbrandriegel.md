# 2. Waldbrandriegel

- Forest fire bars are forest strips up to 300 meters wide, which in the event of a fire turn full fire into more easily combatable ground fires or prevent the spread of ground fires as far as possible and the fire roller withdraw its energy. Forest fire bars can also have a significance for the protection of critical infrastructure or settlement areas.

- Forest fire bars can also have an impact on the protection of critical infrastructure or settlement areas. In order to protect larger forest areas, especially in areas of forest fire hazard class A, such bars can be connected to a system.

- In order to protect larger forest areas especially in areas of forest fire hazard class A, such bars can be connected to a system. In such a system, the main bars run almost perpendicular to the main wind direction.

- In such a system, the main bars run approximately perpendicular to the main wind direction.
