# 3. Brandschutzstreifen

- (Brand) protective strips are usually 25 to 30 m wide with trees and of easily combustible material such as impact compartment, brushwood or drought wood to be kept free. The lower forest authority can arrange the installation and maintenance of fire strips against the forest owners in particular in larger forest areas of forest fire hazard class A according to § 28 para. 1 SächsWaldG.

- According to § 28 para. 1 SächsWaldG, the lower forestry authority can order the installation and maintenance of fire stripes in respect of forest owners, especially in larger forest areas of forest fire hazard class A. This measure is to be applied primarily in particularly fire-prone coniferous forests near settlement, tourist and critical infrastructure, mining follow-up landscapes, urban areas and process protection areas, if it is considered necessary in individual cases.
