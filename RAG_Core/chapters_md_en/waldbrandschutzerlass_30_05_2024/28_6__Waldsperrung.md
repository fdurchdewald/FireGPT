# 6. Waldsperrung

- In accordance with § 13 para. 2 sentence 2 SächsWaldG, if this is necessary in individual cases, the lower forestry authority can block forest areas for reasons of forest fire protection. By blocking the entry into the forest for the purpose of recreation can be said or restricted (e.g. offer of roads).

- By blocking, entry of the forest can be said or restricted for the purpose of recreation (e.g. offer of roads). Regardless of the forest fire hazard level, the arrangement of a blocking of affected and surrounding forest areas can also follow due to a large scale situation, in order to limit forest fire risks in forest areas with already existing high inspection of the BRK units.

- Independently of the forest fire hazard level, the arrangement of a blockage of affected and surrounding forest areas can also follow due to a large scale situation, in order to limit forest fire risks in forest areas with already existing high inspection of the BRK units. Forest lock-ups for reasons of forest fire protection are to be announced in particular in local media (radio, newspaper, internet) about which a short-term notice by the population is possible.

- Forest closures due to forest fire protection are to be announced in particular in local media (radio, newspaper, internet), which enables short-term information by the population. In addition, in the context of the technical and/or personnel possibilities of frequently used forest entrance areas should be informed about the blocking.

- In addition, in the context of the technical and/or personnel possibilities of frequently used forest entrance areas should be informed about the blocking. Through the responsible lower forest authority it is constantly to be checked whether the blocking can be lifted in part or completely.

- The responsible lower forestry authority must constantly check whether the blocking can be partially or completely lifted. All legitimate interests (tourism, recreational use for local population, availability of resources, etc.) must be weighed against each other.
