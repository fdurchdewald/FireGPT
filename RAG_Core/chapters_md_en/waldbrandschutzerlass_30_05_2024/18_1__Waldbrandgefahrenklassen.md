# 1. Waldbrandgefahrenklassen

- The forests of the Free State of Saxony are divided into the following forest fire hazard classes: Forest fire hazard class A - Areas with a high forest fire hazard Forest fire hazard class B - Areas with a medium forest fire hazard Forest fire hazard class C - Areas with a low forest fire hazard The demarcation and representation of the individual forest fire hazard classes is carried out on the basis of the municipalities (www.wald.
