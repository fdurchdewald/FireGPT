# 9.

- Climate-adapted forest management PLUS From the second quarter of 2025, private and municipal forest owners, supported by the BMUV, will be supported by the "Climate-friendly forest management PLUS" programme as part of the action programme Natural Climate Protection (ANK).

- It is designed to promote forest conversion towards stable, species-rich and kli-maan-adapted deciduous mixed forests and, in addition to the existing support programme "Climate-adapted forest management", to provide financial incentives for the provision of additional bio-diversity and climate protection services, such as the designation of habitat trees50, the leaving of dead wood in the forest or the natural forest development in parts of a whale.51
