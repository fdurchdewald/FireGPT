# 01.12.2020

- On the basis of § 54 para. 1 No. 2 and para. 2 Sentence 2 of the Thuringian Fire and Civil Protection Act (ThürBKG), as amended on 5 February 2008 (GVBl. p. 22), as last amended by Article 1 of the Law of 29 June 2018 (GVBl.

- 22), as last amended by Article 1 of the Law of 29 June 2018 (GVBl. p. 317), the Ministry of Home Affairs and the Local Government, in agreement with the Ministry of Finance, after consulting the Internal and Local Committee, in charge of the Budget and Finance Committee: § 1 Organization and tasks of civil protection (1) The counties and county-free cities fulfil the tasks of civil protection according to § 2 para. 2 sentence 2 of the German Civil Protection Act (ThürBKG) within the delegated scope of effect.

- 317), the Ministry of Home Affairs and Local Government, in agreement with the Ministry of Finance, decrees, after consulting the Internal and Local Committee, and the Budget and Finance Committee: § 1 Organization and tasks of Civil Protection (1) The counties and county-free cities fulfil the tasks of Civil Protection in accordance with § 2 para. 2 sentence 2 ThürBKG within the delegated scope. As lower civil protection authorities, they have to take the necessary measures for civil protection within the scope of their tasks.

- As lower civil protection authorities, they have to take the necessary measures for civil protection within the framework of their tasks.
