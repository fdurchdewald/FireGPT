# 3.1

- Training at the site or at the association level After successful training "Truppmann Part 1", an additional training for the control of vegetation fires with the focus on • safety instructions • hazards of the deployment site • operational tactics • use of hand tools • use of fire fighting vehicles is recommended. For this purpose, corresponding documents are provided by the Landesfeuerwehrverband for a training in the Kreis- und Stadtfeuerwehrverbanden.
