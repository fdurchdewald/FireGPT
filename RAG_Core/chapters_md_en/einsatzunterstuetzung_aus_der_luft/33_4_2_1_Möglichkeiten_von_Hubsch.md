# 4.2.1 Möglichkeiten von Hubschraubern im Feuerwehreinsatz

- Helicopters can be used to explore confusing, very dynamic locations, to search for people, animals or dangers, such as fires, and therefore offer a wide spectrum of possibilities for use out of the air. Even the illumination, documentation or warning of persons, is possible out of the air.

- Illuminating, documenting or warning people is also possible from the air. Additional applications are possible due to attachments such as special cameras (e.g. thermal imaging), winches or external load hooks.

- Due to attachments, such as special cameras (e.g. thermal image), rope winches or external load hooks, further application possibilities arise. For the external load hooks there are in addition to the well-known extinguishing water ejection containers, among other things, material transport containers or nets, which can be partially opened from the air.

- For the external load hooks there are not only the well-known extinguishing water ejection containers, but also material transport containers or nets, which can be partially opened from the air. This allows materials such as sand bags or similar to be discarded.

- This allows materials such as sandbags or similar to be discarded. Also banal things such as cargo or passenger transports can be of fundamental importance in certain application scenarios.
