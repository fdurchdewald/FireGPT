# 4.1.3 Anforderungswege von UAS

- Units with unmanned aircraft can generally be requested directly by the Operations Manager. This is done via the Integrated Control Centres (ILS), which have deposited drone units in the Operations Control System.

- This is done via the Integrated Control Centres (ILS), which have deposited drone units in the operational control system. During the punctual searches it could be determined that these units are distributed across all districts and major cities or are located in close proximity.

- During the point-by-point research it could be determined that these units are distributed across all districts and large cities or are present in close proximity. This usually results in short approaches.

- The request of a THW TrUL unit is made, as is customary with all other THW units, via the appropriate local or regional association or through the specialist advice of THW.
