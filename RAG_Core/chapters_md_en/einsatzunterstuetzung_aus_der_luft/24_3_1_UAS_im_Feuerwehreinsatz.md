# 3.1 UAS im Feuerwehreinsatz

- The author brought together possibilities for the use of UAS in fire brigade deployment by means of research from previous specialist papers, current publications in journals and new studies. Introductors of a drone squadron of THW were available for a discussion about the current structure of the drone squadrons as well as possible applications within these units.
