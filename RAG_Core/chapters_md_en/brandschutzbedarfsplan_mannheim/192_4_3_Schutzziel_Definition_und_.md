# 4.3 Schutzziel-Definition und Planungsgrundlagen

- Introduction and fundamental considerations Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 102 The dispatch time (= time from the acceptance of the emergency call in the control centre to the alarm of the fire brigade) is not influenced by the fire protection requirements planning, since the emergency call reception and processing (= dispatch time) is carried out by the control centre and thus the dispatch time depends on the organisation of the control centre. Therefore, the term "help period", which usually includes the dispatch time in the control centre, is not used to define the protection objective.

- Therefore, the term "help period", which generally includes the scheduling time in the control centre, is not used to define the protection objective. However, in the demand plan only the so-called "recipient times" are used.

- In the demand plan, however, only the so-called "meeting times" are used. The time to arrive is the time period from the alarming of the fire brigade to the arrival at the deployment site.

- The arrival time is the time from the alarming of the fire brigade to the arrival at the site. In the protection target for the "critical apartment fire" is also between the 1st and the 2nd.

- In addition, the protection goal for the "critical apartment fire" distinguishes between the 1st and 2nd meeting time.
