# 5.2   Standortstruktur der Berufsfeuerwehr

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 127 的 As a result of urban planning changes and the associated location task of the main fire station Mitte, the location structure of the fire stations and fire brigades was intensively investigated and discussed within the project.

- A planned structure of the fire brigade with two fire guards is not recommended from a needs planning point of view, since the level of supply in the area of the focus of application in the inner city area and the risk coverage in total would significantly worsen compared to the IST state.

- A maintenance of the "three fire wake structure" is necessary from the point of view of demand planning. Building on this finding, various possibilities were examined within the city and as a result, a property was found in the Neckarauer Straße ("Vögele-Geände") as a result of the location of the new fire station Mitte.
