# 3.4 Struktur und Verfügbarkeit der Freiwilligen Feuerwehr

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 85 In addition to the personnel, a fleet to be set up depending on the local risks and occurrences is required for effective and reliable handling of operations.The vehicle measurement depends on different factors, such as: - hazard potential - use scenarios for design - operation events in general - availability, structure and integration of the volunteer fire brigade - fire-fighting water supply / hydrant net - possibilities of community support This section contains a brief presentation of the vehicles and technology in the IST state at the fire brigade Mannheim and gives an insight into current planning in this area.
