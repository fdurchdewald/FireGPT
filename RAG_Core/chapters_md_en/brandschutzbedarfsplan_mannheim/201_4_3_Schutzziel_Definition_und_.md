# 4.3 Schutzziel-Definition und Planungsgrundlagen

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 108 Protective objective K1: Critical housing fire "B3 and B4" The qualitative goal is that the fire brigade is in the area of danger classes B3 and B4 in the critical housing fire: within 10 minutes after the alarming with 12 Fu (functions) and after a further 5 minutes (10 + 5 = 15 minutes) with a further 6 Fu (12 Fu + 6 Fu = 18 Fu) at the site.

- Degree of achievement The quantitative objective is a total achievement of 90 % in relation to the sum of the operations according to the protection objective. Definition of protection objective: "Critical housing fire" Planning basis "Critical housing fire in areas B3 and B4" Notes: Almost 90% of the built-up area in the city area of Mannheim are classified into hazard classes B3 & B4.
