# 3.3 Funktionsbesetzungsplan der Berufsfeuerwehr

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 79 The members of the volunteer fire brigade play an important role in addition to the full-time forces of the professional fire brigade. In addition to the use as supplementary units for the professional fire brigade, the departments of the volunteer fire brigade are used in particular for large-scale damage, mass attack of events and for the taking over of special tasks.
