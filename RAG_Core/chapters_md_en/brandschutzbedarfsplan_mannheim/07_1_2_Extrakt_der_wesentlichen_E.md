# 1.2 Extrakt der wesentlichen Ergebnisse

- Fire protection requirements plan of the City of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 10 extract Impact on personnel requirements (human resources management) To determine the personnel requirements of the professional fire brigade, which results from fire protection requirements planning, the personnel economic parameters were analyzed and updated.By implementing the recommended measures of fire protection requirements plan, such as interlinking the control centre with the emergency service, the unification of the tactical concept of basic protection and the demand planning design of the special functions, there is a personnel need of 19 VZE.

- By implementing the recommended measures of the fire protection demand plan, such as interlinking the control centre with the emergency service, the unification of the tactical concept of basic protection and the need planning design of the special functions, there is a staff need of 19 VTAE. The updating of the personnel economic parameters results in an additional need of 7 VTAE.

- The update of the personnel management parameters results in an additional requirement of 7 FTE. Under the basis of the functional occupation according to recommendations of the fire protection requirements plan and the updated personnel management parameters, a staff increase in the sum of 26 FTE results (increase of 9% compared to the IST position plan with 293.5 FTE).

- In accordance with the recommendations of the fire protection requirements plan and the updated personnel management parameters, a staff increase of 26 FTE (an increase of 9% compared to the 1ST position plan of 293.5 FTE) resulted in this. The project identified alternative actions in the area of human resources management, which are examined and evaluated internally in the context of the implementation phase.
