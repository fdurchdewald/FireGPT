# 5.1  Beschreibung der Ableitung der SOLL- Empfehlung

- Hazard potential: Special objects Approach Demand planning Differentiated consideration according to RISIKO distribution (2) Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 118

- The requirements for fire brigades with regard to arrival times, functional strengths and qualifications are sometimes higher than the requirements according to the application scenario "Critical Home Fire". Some of these objects / areas require a specific consideration: Specific application scenarios: "ABC-Betriebs-Betrieb" & "Establishment Fire Clinic" Consequently, the requirements planning and the resulting location structure and the functional occupation of the professional fire brigade are based not only on the classical scenario "Critical Home Fire" but also on further specific deployment scenarios.

- Some of these objects/areas require a specific consideration: Specific application scenarios: "ABC-Einsatz" & "Establishment fire Klinikum" Consequently, the requirements planning and the resulting location structure and the functional occupation of the occupational fire brigade are based not only on the classical scenario "Critical home fire" but also on further specific application scenarios.

- The term "risk" combines the risk potential with the probability of use. The recommendation of the guard structure is not only based on the hazard potential but also on the distribution of the application sites: Analysis of the distribution of the application sites across the city area.
