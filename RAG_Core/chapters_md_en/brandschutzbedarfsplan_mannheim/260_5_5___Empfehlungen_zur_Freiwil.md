# 5.5   Empfehlungen zur Freiwilligen Feuerwehr

- Fire protection requirements plan of the City of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 153 Standby Group On the basis of the planning basis, the preparedness group is not mandatory (the related financial compensation could then be regulated otherwise, for example by means of an effort compensation). However, the maintenance of the preparedness group could also be discussed in order to preserve the motivation of the volunteers.

- In order to preserve the motivation of the volunteers' forces, the maintenance of the standby group could also be discussed. The advantage of the standby group is undisputed that for a tactical unit of the volunteer fire brigade there is a 100% reliability in terms of availability (this is naturally not given in case of a classic alarm from the "incidental availability").

- The advantage of the preparedness group is undisputed that for a tactical unit of the volunteer fire brigade there is a 100% reliability in terms of availability (this is naturally not given in case of a classic alarm from the "incidental availability").
