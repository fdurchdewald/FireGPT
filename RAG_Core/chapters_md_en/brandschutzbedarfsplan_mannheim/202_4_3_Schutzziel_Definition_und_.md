# 4.3 Schutzziel-Definition und Planungsgrundlagen

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 109 Protective objective K2: Critical housing fire "B1 and B2" The qualitative goal is that the fire brigade should be at the site with a further 12 Fu (6 Fu + 12 Fu = 18 Fu) within 10 minutes after the alarm with 6 Fu (functions) and after a further 5 minutes (10 + 5 = 15 minutes) with a further 12 Fu (6 Fu + 12 Fu = 18 Fu).

- Degree of achievement The quantitative objective is a total achievement rate of 90 % in relation to the sum of the operations according to the protection objective. Note: For fire brigade requirements planning, the term "time of arrival" is used.

- Note: The term "time to arrive" is used for firefighters' needs planning. "Auxiliary period" also includes the time of emergency call reception in the control centre or emergency centre.

- The "help period" also includes the time of emergency call reception in the control centre or emergency centre. However, because the location planning of the fire brigade is subject to different laws than the influence of the dispatch time in the control centre, the "residence time" (ETZ) is used for security planning, which includes the time span from the alarm of the forces to the arrival (the arrival) at the emergency center.
