Many of the commuters are available for missions, but have a long way to go to the equipment house [alarming for large and long-term deployments].

- Many of the disembarks are available for missions, but have a long journey to the equipment house [alarming for large and lengthy missions]. Overall, the availability of the volunteer fire brigade is limited during the day.
