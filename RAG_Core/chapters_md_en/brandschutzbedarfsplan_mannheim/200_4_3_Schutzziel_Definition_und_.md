# 4.3 Schutzziel-Definition und Planungsgrundlagen

- Fire protection requirements plan of the city of Mannheim, as of: 04.04.2013 107 Explanation of the parameter "Target achievement degree" (ZEG) The target achievement degree (ZEG) is the percentage of the assignments, which meets the criteria with regard to the arrival time and the set functional strength. A target achievement degree of 90% means that 90 out of 100 assignments have achieved the required functional strength in the correct arrival time.

- A target achievement rate of 90% means that 90 out of 100 inserts have achieved the required functional strength in the right time. For the 10 remaining inserts, at least one criterion has not been met.

- At least one criterion has not been met in the 10 other missions. The CEC is dependent on various factors, such as: - Simultaneity of operations - Structure and potential for danger in the urban area - Claims for the optimization of the personnel assignment - "External influences such as weather and traffic conditions In the context of the demand planning, we agreed on the degree of achievement as a target agreement between the fire brigade Mannheim and the political decision-makers.
