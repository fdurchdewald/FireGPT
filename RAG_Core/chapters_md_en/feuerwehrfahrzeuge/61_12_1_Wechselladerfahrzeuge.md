# 12.1 Wechselladerfahrzeuge

- The interchangeable loader vehicles (WLF) according to DIN 14505 are vehicles for the transport of roll-off containers and consist of a chassis with cab for the reception of a crew and a fixed loader device mounted on the chassis, which is used for the transport of the respective roll-off container. The crew consists of at least one driver and a machinist.

- The crew consists of at least one driver and one machinist. Alternate loader vehicles may also be equipped with their own loader crane (WLFK).

- Alternator vehicles can also be equipped with their own loader crane (WLFK). Alternator loader systems are only available if coherent concepts are submitted to the governments and the economic viability of the planned measure compared to the procurement of conventional fire fighting equipment is demonstrated.

- Rechargeable loader systems are only available if coherent concepts are submitted to governments and the economic viability of the planned measure compared to the procurement of conventional fire fighting equipment is demonstrated. A WLF "in short" has two axles and a WLF "lang" has three or four axles.

- A WLF "short" has two axles and a WLF "long" has three or four axles. The radio call indicators of changeable loader driving are: 35 WLF short / WLFK short 12 NIGHT VEHICLES 36 WLF long / WLFK long Supply vehicles are fire trucks for the transport of equipment, extinguishing equipment and other goods for the supply of used units.
