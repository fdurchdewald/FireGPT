# 3.2	 Kraftfahrzeug-Kategorien

- All motor vehicles are classified by one of the following three categories, depending on their driving characteristics on the road or on the terrain: z Category 1 = roadworthy motor vehicle normally suitable for use on paved roads z Category 2 = off-road motor vehicle suitable for use on all roads and conditionally on off-road driving z Category 3 = off-road motor vehicle suitable for use on all roads and for off-road driving (cross-country driving)
