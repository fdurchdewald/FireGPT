# 13.3.4  Tragkraftspritzen-Anhänger

- The TSA load-bearing syringe trailer is a specially designed, single-axle firefighter trailer with a minimum fire-fighting load for a fire-fighting group 1/8 including a PFPN 10-1000 load-bearing syringe according to DIN EN 14 466. It mainly serves fire-fighting in the local / district area and forms an independent tactical unit with the accompanying team.
