# 10.2  Mehrzweckfahrzeug

- Multipurpose vehicles (MZF) according to the "Technical description of construction" are mainly used for the establishment of a management post as well as for the transport of crew and equipment. In the MZF guide middle (west, writing equipment, etc.) should be carried.

- In the MZF, leadership should be carried with medium (west, writing equipment, etc.). The MZF is only supported if the fire brigade has at least one firefighting group, or an auxiliary fire-fighting group.

- The MZF will only be funded if the fire brigade has at least one extinguishing group or emergency fire group vehicle. Radio call code: 11
