# 5.1.3	Mittlere Löschfahrzeuge

- The MLF is a fire-fighting vehicle according to DIN 14530-25 with a fire-fighting centrifugal pump driven by the vehicle. The equipment includes a device for fast water discharge, a fire-fighting water tank and a fire-weir-technical loading for a group (1/8).

- The equipment includes a device for fast water discharge, an extinguishing water tank and a fire-weir technical loading for a group (1/8). The crew consists of a squadron (1/5), which forms an independent tactical unit with the crew added to the group.

- The crew consists of a squadron (1/5), which forms an independent tactical unit with the crew added to the group. The vehicle is mainly used for fire fighting.
