- Small extinguishing vehicle Other extinguishing vehicles combine all fire-fighting vehicles which cannot be assigned to one of the existing categories. These are also not standardised and are adapted to the needs of the operator.

- These are also not standardised and are adapted to the needs of the operator.The small extinguishing vehicle (KLF) according to DIN 14530-24 is a vehicle with a load-bearing syringe, a direction for fast water discharge, a extinguishing water tank and a fire-wehr technical load for a group (1/8), whose crew consists of a squadron (1/5).
