# 10.3  Einsatzleitwagen ELW 1

- Operations management vehicles (ELW 1) according to DIN SPEC 14507-2 serve the operational management primarily z for the journey and exploration of deployments. z are tools for the management of tactical units z as aids for the management of associations with guidance aids, but without staff-like guidance The ELW 1 is only supported if the fire-fighting unit has at least two trains according to FwDV 3. A promotion is also carried out, provided that the fire brigade is provided with a stationing concept for the operational management with corresponding tasks.

- Funding is also provided if the fire brigade is provided for the operational management with corresponding tasks in a stationing concept created at the district level. Radio call index: 12
