# 3.1

- New strategy for the protection of the population With the "Law on Civil Protection and the Federal Disaster Assistance" (ZSKG), which came into force in April 2009, structural consequences of the "New strategy for the protection of the population in Germany" agreed jointly by the federal government and the Länder were drawn and a simple legal basis for the Federal Disaster Assistance was created. The decisive content of the "New strategy for the protection of the population in Germany" is the realization that the threat of the Federal Republic of Germany is derived less from the acts of war of another state, but from multi-national terrorist groups, major damage events and disasters.
