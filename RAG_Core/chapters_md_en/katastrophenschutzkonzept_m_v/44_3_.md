# 3.

- The Federal Government The constitutional civil protection competence pursuant to Article 73(1) of the Basic Law (GG) imposes an obligation on the Federal Government, independent of day-to-day political developments, to ensure the possible use of civil protection funds at any time. Building on this responsibility for the protection of the population in the event of defence, the Federal Government regularly assists the Länder in dealing with disasters and major damage situations, regardless of their cause.

In the case of civil protection, just as the Federal Government is allowed to rely on the civil protection institutions of the Länder, the Länder can rely on the Federal Agency for Civil Protection.
