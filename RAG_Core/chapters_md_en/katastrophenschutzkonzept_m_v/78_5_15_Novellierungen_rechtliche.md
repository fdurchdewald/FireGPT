# 5.15 Novellierungen rechtlicher Grundlagen

- The legal foundations of civil protection in Mecklenburg-Vorpommern are to be amended due to the listed new areas of responsibility. The Landeskastrophesschutzgesetz has proven itself and has to be adapted accordingly.

- The State Disaster Protection Act has proven its worth and needs to be adapted accordingly. 42 Ministry of the Interior and Sports Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania Unit II 450 In implementing the individual measures, the data protection issues have to be appreciated and taken into account in the necessary legislative procedures.

- 42 Ministry of the Interior and Sports Mecklenburg-Vorpommern Unit II 450 The following decrees, agreements and administrative provisions must be amended as a matter of priority in the implementation of the individual measures: - Administrative provision for the reorganisation of civil protection, - Principles of cooperation between ministries in special situations of danger, - Administrative provision for the issuing and passing on of warnings, notifications and information in the event of disasters, large-scale threats and other events in need of coordination, - Staff Regulations ImFüSt/AStIM, - Decree on official threats, - Agreement on mutual assistance in the event of disasters and major disasters Poland – Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, Saxony, Brandenburg.
