- State of play The equipment provided by the federal government has been and will in principle be assigned to the KSE in accordance with the administrative regulation for the reorganization of civil protection in Mecklenburg-Vorpommern of 17 January 1997 and is still available to the lower civil protection authorities for the management of major damage situations and disasters. Since the implementation of the new equipment concept of the federal government has partially removed existing equipment elements from the concept, this non-conceptual equipment has been taken over in coordination with the lower civil protection authorities by the state.
