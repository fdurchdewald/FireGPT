- Networked World/Critical Infrastructures In addition to the asymmetric threat situation, the vulnerability of highly technical and networked, complex societies is added as a new real source of danger. Due to the extreme dependence on infrastructural services, society has become very vulnerable, and these vulnerabilities have increased greatly not only due to external threats, but also due to the high dependencies between the individual infrastructure systems in the interior.
