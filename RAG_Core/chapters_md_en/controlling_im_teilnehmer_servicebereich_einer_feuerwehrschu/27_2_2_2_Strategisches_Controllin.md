# 2.2.2 Strategisches Controlling

- Strategic controlling is geared towards the medium to long-term success of an enterprise and is oriented towards the following questions: - "What are the objectives pursued?" - "Which paths are pursued to achieve goals?" - "Where does the enterprise want to stand in five or ten years' time?" To answer these questions systematically, the use of the two instruments mentioned below is recommended.
