# 4.2   Waldbrandeinsatzkarten

- 1For the effective fight against forest fire, forest fire maps must be made available to the integrated control centres or first alarming sites in fire and civil protection and to the fire brigades in particularly fire-prone forest areas, preferably as digital application maps with Gauss-Krüger coordinate system. 2The development, coordination, production and distribution of suitable maps is carried out by the governments.

- 2The development, coordination, production and distribution of suitable maps is carried out by the governments. 3Depending on the local hazard assessment, the following information is required in the forest fire intervention maps: – roads and paths that can be driven by heavy trucks in single or double lanes, both inside and outside the forest; • clearly designated collection points for the emergency personnel; • particularly important access routes from public roads; • possibly evasive and turning points; – suitable water collection points – also outside the forest; • waters suitable for water extraction by helicopters with external extinguishing water load tanks; • other open waters; • hydrants.

- 3Depending on the local hazard assessment, the following information is required in the forest fire intervention maps: – roads and roads that can be driven by single or double-lane heavy goods vehicles, inside and outside the forest, • clearly designated collection points for the emergency personnel, • in particular important access routes from public roads, • possibly alternative and turning points, – suitable water extraction points – also outside the forest, • waters that are suitable for water extraction by helicopters with extinguishing water external load containers, • other open waters, • hydrants. 4The necessary forest fire-specific map information for the forest fire intervention maps are collected and passed on to the governments by the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forestry and the Forestry Operations of the Bavarian State Forests – possibly with the support of the management of the local fire brigades.

- 4The necessary forest fire-specific map information for the forest fire intervention maps are collected by the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forests and the forest operations of the Bavarian State Forests – if necessary with the support of the management of the local fire brigades – and passed on to the governments. 5By mutual exchange of information and maps of the Integrated Control Centres or first alarming places in fire and disaster protection, fire brigades, lower forest authorities and forest operations of the Bavarian State Forests is to facilitate the finding of locations for forest fire fighting.
