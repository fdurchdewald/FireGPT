# 9.   Berichterstattung und Waldbrandstatistik

- 1Forest fires of a size of five hectares or more must be immediately reported to the State Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry by the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forestry. 2Fires that threaten to expand to disaster must be notified by the competent civil protection authority directly by telephone to the competent government and the situation centre of Bavaria in the State Ministry of the Interior (see point 6.2).

- 2In addition, fires that threaten to spread to disaster must be notified by the competent civil protection authority directly by telephone to the competent government and the Bavarian Situation Centre in the State Ministry of the Interior (see point 6.2). 3For statistical purposes, the Bavarian Forestry Administration conducts a forest fire statistics.

- 3For statistical purposes, the Bavarian Forestry Administration conducts a forest fire statistics. 4For this purpose, the civil protection authorities will send a copy of the fire report to the local authority responsible for food, agriculture and forestry immediately.
