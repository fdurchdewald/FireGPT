# 5.3   Früherkennung

- 1In the event of a high or very high risk of forest fire (stages 4 and 5 of the German Weather Service's risk of forest fire), surveillance flights for early detection of forest fires are carried out in the affected areas in cooperation with the German Air Observation Service (Deutsche Flugobservierungsdienst, Luftrettungsstauffel Bayern e. V.).

- V. Monitoring flights for early detection of forest fires. 2In this case, trained persons designated by the civil protection authorities, the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forestry and the forestry operations of the Bavarian State Forests are used for air observation.

- 2The civil protection authorities, the offices for food, agriculture and forests and the forestry enterprises of the Bavarian State Forests will use trained persons for air observation. 3The flights will be arranged by the civil protection authorities at the initiative of the Bavarian Forest Administration (through the responsible offices for food, agriculture and forests).
