# 2.   Rechtsgrundlagen

- Legal bases for this Directive include the following provisions in the current version: – Forest Act for Bavaria (BayWaldG) of 22 July 2005 (GVBl p. 313, BayRS 7902-1-L) – Bavarian Civil Protection Act (BayKSG) of 24 July 1996 (GVBl p. 282, BayRS 215-4-1-I) – Bavarian Fire Brigade Act – BayFwG – (BayRS 215-3-1-I) – Ordinance implementing the Bavarian Fire Brigade Act – AVBayFwG – (BayRS 215-3-1-1-I) – Announcement of the Bavarian State Ministry of the Interior of 14 June 1993 (AllMBl p. 856) – Announcement of the Bavarian State Ministry of the Interior on alarming in rescue service, fire and disaster protection in Bavaria (Alarming Notice – ABek) of 14 June 1993 (AllMBl p. 856)
