# 5.4   Waldbrandwarnung durch den Deutschen Wetterdienst

- 1In the event of an acute forest fire risk, the Agrarmeteorological Branch Office of the German Weather Service in Weihenstephan will also send a request for corresponding radio announcements to the radio stations connected to the traffic warning service. 2The relevant police departments and governments, the State Ministry of the Interior, the State Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry as well as the responsible Integrated Control Centres or first alarming bodies in the fire and civil protection receive the forest fire warnings for information.
