# 5.1   Waldbrandwarnung durch die Forstbehörden

- 1In the event of an increased risk of forest fire, the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forestry publish regional and the Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry nationwide forest fire warnings. 2The population is informed about this via the media and asked to be particularly careful.

- 2The population is informed about this via the media and asked to behave particularly cautiously. 3Forest owners are advised not to burn rice and crown material during the processing of wood, but to transport or bake.

- 3Forest owners are advised not to burn, but to transport, or to pick up, rice and crown material during the processing of wood. 4In case of acute forest fire danger, warning signs can also be used locally to inform the population.
