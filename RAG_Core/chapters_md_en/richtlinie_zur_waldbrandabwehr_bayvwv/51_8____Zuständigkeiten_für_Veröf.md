# 8.   Zuständigkeiten für Veröffentlichungen

- 1The German Weather Service provides regular information on the risk of forest fire. 2The forest fire warning in case of increased risk of forest fire is initiated by the State Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry or the affected offices for food, agriculture and forests (see point 5.1).

- 2The forest fire warning in the event of an increased risk of forest fire is initiated by the State Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Forestry or by the affected offices for food, agriculture and forestry (see point 5.1). 3The media are informed about forest fires by the county authorities, in the event of major forest fires by the competent government or the State Ministry of the Interior.
