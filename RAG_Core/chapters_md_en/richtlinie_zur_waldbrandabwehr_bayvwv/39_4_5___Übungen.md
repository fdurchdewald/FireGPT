# 4.5   Übungen

- 1The local fire brigades and/or fire brigade management services are informed about the local infrastructure and the conditions of use. 2They are supported by the offices for food, agriculture and forests and the forestry businesses of the Bavarian State Forests.

- 2They are supported by the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forests and the Forestry Operations of the Bavarian State Forests. 3In highly fire-prone forest areas, joint forest fire exercises by the civil protection authorities, the fire brigades, the police, the other intervention organizations, the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forests, the Forestry Operations of the Bavarian State Forests and, if necessary, private and municipal forest owners promote effective fire fighting in an emergency.

- 3In highly fire-prone forest areas, joint forest fire exercises by civil protection authorities, fire brigades, police forces, other intervention organisations, the Offices for Food, Agriculture and Forestry, the forestry businesses of the Bavarian State Forests and, if necessary, private and municipal forest owners promote effective fire fighting in an emergency. 4Essential contents are the deployment tactics in forest fires, the securing of fire water supply, the cooperation in the operational management and the improvement of local knowledge.
