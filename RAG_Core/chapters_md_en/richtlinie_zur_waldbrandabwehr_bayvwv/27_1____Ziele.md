# 1.   Ziele

- 1Especially with regard to climate change and the associated increase in dry periods, an increase in the risk of forest fires is expected. 2The forest fire prevention directive serves to coordinate the activities of the civil protection authorities, the Integrated Control Centres, the fire brigades, the municipalities, the German Weather Service, the lower forest authorities (Ämter für Ernährung, Landwirtschaft und Forsten) and the forestry enterprises of the Bavarian State Forests.

- 2The Forest Fire Prevention Directive is designed to coordinate the activities of the civil protection authorities, the Integrated Control Centres, the fire brigades, the municipalities, the German Weather Service, the lower forest authorities (Ämter für Ernährung, Landwirtschaft und Forsten) and the forestry businesses of the Bavarian State Forests. 3It thus ensures the smooth running of prevention, early detection and effective control of forest fires.
