- § 18a Personenausberichtsstelle (1) The personenausberichtsstelle (section 13 sentence 2 point 5) may process personal data to the extent required for the purpose of searching for missing persons and family reunification in Katastrophen (section 1 paragraph 1) and large damage situations if facts justify the assumption of the impairment of a large number of persons. A large damage situation is an event, the life or health of a large number of people, significant material values or the environment endangered or impairs or the effective fight against which the forces and means of security provided in the normal service are not sufficient and in which a supralocal or cen- tral operation is necessary.

- A major damage situation is an occurrence which endangers or affects the life or health of a large number of people, significant physical assets or the environment, the effective fight against which the forces and means of security provided in the regular service are not sufficient and in which a supra-regional or cen- tral operation is necessary.
