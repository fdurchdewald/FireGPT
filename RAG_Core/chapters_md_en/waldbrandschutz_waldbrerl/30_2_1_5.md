# 2.1.5

- In order to ensure the readiness of the forest to operate, all measures of preventive forest fire protection, such as the preparation of operational and alarm documents, the verification of forest fire control plants, extinguishing water sampling points and forest fire detection technology, the creation and maintenance of wound strips, and the training and training of forestry staff, must be carried out annually by 1 March under the responsibility of forest authorities and forest owners.
