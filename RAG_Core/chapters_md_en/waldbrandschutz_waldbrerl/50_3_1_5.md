# 3.1.5

- The LU appoints the Kreiswaldbrandschutzbeauftragte and its representatives on the proposal of the forest authorities from the circle of the heads of the Leitforstamten for the forest fire protection according to § 15 WaldBrSchVO. The Kreiswaldbrandschutzbeauftragte or the Kreiswaldbrandschutzbeauftragte is to be a member of the advisory staff of the lower civil protection authority.

- The district fire protection officer or the district fire protection officer shall be a member of the advisory staff of the lower civil protection authority. The advisory staff shall be formed by the lower civil protection authority in accordance with § 11 LKatSG M-V.
