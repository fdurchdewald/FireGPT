# 3.1.2

- All counties and county-free cities with high and medium forest fire risk (A and B) carry out an annual consultation with the Waldbrandschutz AG before the start of the forest fire season. In particular, the forest fire events of the past year have to be evaluated and measures for the prevention and control of forest fires, including special intervention plans for the current year, have to be defined.

- In particular, the forest fire events of the past year must be evaluated as well as measures to prevent and combat forest fires, including special deployment plans for the current year. This approach has also proven itself in counties and non-circular cities with low forest fire risk (C) and should be continued (§ 20 paragraph 4 WaldBrSchVO).
