# 3.3.1

- Operations management below the disaster If, in addition to the ÖFW, further forces and funds are involved in the measures of forest fire control (e.g. Bundeswehr, police, THW) and a coordination of the partial forces becomes necessary, the supervisory authority can take over the operations management. According to § 18 paragraph 4 in conjunction with § 27 BrSchG, this is the responsible Landrat or Oberbürgermeister.
