# 3.6.1

- The preparation and updating of the forest fire intervention maps for the entire forest of the country takes place according to § 18 in conjunction with § 6 paragraph 5 WaldBrSchVO by the LFoA. The basis of the digital forest fire intervention maps are the geobase data of the official surveying system.

- The basis of the digital forest fire maps are the geobase data of the official surveying system. The forest fire maps are printed in digital and analog form as overview maps in scale 1 : 50 000 with "Universal Transversal Mercator Projection Version (UTM-Gitter).

- The forest fire cards are printed in digital and analogue form as overview cards in scale 1 : 50 000 with "Universal Transversal Mercator Projection Version (UTM-Gitter)" . If required, additional maps in scale 1 : 25 000 or 1 : 10 000 can be obtained from the LFoA.
