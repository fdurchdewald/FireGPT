# 3.4.3

- The agreement on mutual assistance in the event of disasters and serious accidents between the Minister for Home Affairs and Public Administration of the Republic of Poland and the Ministry of Home Affairs and Sports of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern of 18 July 2002 regulates the cooperation between the two countries. The agreement shall be used if the authorities responsible in the territory of the Republic of Poland and the State of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern consider it necessary to support their own actions through forces and funds from the territory of the Party.
