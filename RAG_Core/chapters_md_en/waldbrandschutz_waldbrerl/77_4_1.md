# 4.1

- In accordance with § 6 paragraph 4 WaldBrSchVO, an extinguished fire site is to be secured by the forest owner (forest fire aftercare), ensuring that no new forest fire arises from an extinguished fire site.

- It is necessary to ensure that no new forest fire arises from an extinguished fire site. If a forest owner is not to be reached or unknown, the forest authority will take over this task in accordance with § 13 paragraph 3 WaldBrSchVO.
