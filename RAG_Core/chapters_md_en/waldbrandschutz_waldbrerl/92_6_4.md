- Information The information on fire-proof behaviour is provided in accordance with § 10 WaldBrSchVO. 7 Public Relations In addition to the information of the population by radio, television, press, internet and local notice about the current forest fire hazard (forest fire hazard level) and the associated dangers, the forest authorities have to take care by means of the local press and distribution of information materials targeted public relations work for the further clarification of the population according to § 11 WaldBrSchVO.

- 7 Public relations In addition to the information provided by the public through radio, television, press, internet and local announcements on the current forest fire hazard (forest fire hazard level) and the associated risks, the forest authorities have to provide targeted public relations work for further clarification of forest fire risk in accordance with § 11 WaldBrSchVO through the use of the local press and distribution of information materials. All other authorities and institutions involved in the protection of forest fire risk at least from the forest fire hazard level.
