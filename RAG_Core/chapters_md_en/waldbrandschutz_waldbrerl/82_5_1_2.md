# 5.1.2

- Integrated control centres The integrated control centres shall immediately report, after alerting the forces, all forest fires not reported by forest owners or forest services during duty periods to the permanent FoA or NPA and outside duty hours to or from the relevant on-call service. This procedure is necessary to ensure the rapid supply of forest services to support forest fire control.

- This procedure is necessary to ensure the rapid supply of forest forces to support forest fire control. Furthermore, the integrated forest fire control centres report immediately to the LZ IM from 1 hectare onwards.
