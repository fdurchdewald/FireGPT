# 3.4.1

- According to § 15 paragraph 3 LKatSG M-V, the lower civil protection authority detects the occurrence and the end of a disaster. If the disaster acts beyond the competence of the lower civil protection authority, reference is made to § 16 paragraph 2 LKatSG M-V.

- If the disaster acts beyond the competence of the lower civil protection authority, reference is made to § 16 paragraph 2 LKatSG M-V. The lower civil protection authority uses the expert knowledge of the ÖFW and the relevant forest authority.
