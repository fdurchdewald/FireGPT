# 3.3.2

- Operations management in the event of a disaster In the event of a disaster according to § 15 LKatSG M-V, § 21 Paragraph 2 WaldBrSchVO in conjunction with § 16 Paragraph 1 LKatSG M-V applies.

- After that, the lower civil protection authority takes over the management of the Einsat- zes. The advisory or administrative staff of the lower civil protection authority uses for the implementation of the operational-tactical operations of the technical operational management or the management staff, which is formed from the technical operational management.

- The advisory or administrative staff of the lower civil protection authority makes use of the technical operations management or the management staff, which is formed from the technical operations management, to carry out the operational-tactical operations. The technical and tactical measures are the responsibility of the local operations management or the operational manager in accordance with § 18 BrSchG.

- The technical tactical measures are the responsibility of the local operational management or the operational manager in accordance with § 18 BrSchG. If a third party assistance service (e.g. THW, Bundeswehr) is mandatory, the procedure is according to § 16 paragraph 2 LKatSG M-V.
