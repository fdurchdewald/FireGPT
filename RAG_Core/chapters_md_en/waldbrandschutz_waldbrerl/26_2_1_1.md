# 2.1.1

- The implementation and financing of forestry-technical measures, such as the creation of wound and protective strips as well as forest fire bars, the maintenance of a road network suitable for extinguishing and rescue vehicles as well as the provision and storage of forest fire control bodies regulates §§ 6 to 10 WaldBrSchVO. § 12 and for forest fire control plants § 14 WaldBrSchVO apply for extinguishing water sampling points in forests.
