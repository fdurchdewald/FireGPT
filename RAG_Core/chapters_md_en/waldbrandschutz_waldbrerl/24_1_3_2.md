# 1.3.2

- The forestry authority and the civil protection authority shall inform the other department of any further administrative provisions relating to forest fire protection. The forest authority shall provide the forest authorities with further details on points 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4.2, 2.4.3, 3.1, 3.3.3, 3.6, 3.7, 4, 5.1.1, 5.2.1 and 6.4.

- For the forestry authorities, further details of points 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4.2, 2.4.3, 3.1, 3.3.3, 3.6, 3.7, 4, 5.1.1, 5.2.1 and 6.4.2.
