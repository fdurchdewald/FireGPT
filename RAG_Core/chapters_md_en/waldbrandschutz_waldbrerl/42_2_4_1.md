# 2.4.1

- Forest fire monitoring systems The manager or the head of the forest authority is responsible for the occupation of the existing forest fire monitoring systems after the triggering of the forest fire risk stages. Through the responsible management forest office, the cooperation and the exchange of information in forest fire reporting from forest fire monitoring systems with the federal forest farms and the neighbouring federal states is to be ensured.

- In addition, in the framework of the existing agreement between the LU and the Regional Directorate for Forests Szczecin of 25 July 2006 on forest protection with a focus on forest fire protection, a forest-side cross-border alert has to be developed with the Regional Directorate.
