# 2.4 Führung des Einsatzabschnittes „Luft“

- Tactical guidance for use from the air means: 的 Right-time requirement (based on a fire development forecast, for example, the simple tactical forest fire forecast according to CAMPBELL, page 20 2005) of the right aircraft (see division of the aircraft in Klassen). 的 Use of the aircraft in the right way (strategy and tactics).

- Use of the aircraft in the correct way (strategy and tactics). 的 Sufficient aircraft in the air (overtimes, including consideration of water fillings on the ground, tank stops, crew changes, etc.).

- Adequate aircraft in the air (overtimes, including consideration of water fillings on the ground, tank stops, crew changes, etc.). Adequate planning of the water absorption, addition of netting agents and later flame retardants, so-called retardants – optimisation of the flight routes and water intake points, in order to achieve rapid circulation.

- Planning of the water absorption, addition of netting agents and later flame retardants, so-called retardants – optimisation of the flight routes and water absorption points in order to achieve rapid circulation. In use, a stop of private aircraft transport (including drones, hang-gliders, parachute jumpers or similar) must be carried out immediately in the field and all landing, arrival and departure areas.

- In use, there must be an immediate interruption of private aircraft transport (including drones, hang-gliders, parachute jumpers or similar) in the field of operation and in all landing, arrival and departure areas. This is most easily done by the requirement and imposition of a flight limitation offer (ED-R).
