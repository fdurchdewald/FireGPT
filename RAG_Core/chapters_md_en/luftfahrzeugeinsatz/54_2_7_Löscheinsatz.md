# 2.7 Löscheinsatz

- Aircraft can be used in the soldering unit as follows: a) Direct soldering unit (with and without netting agent or gel forming agent) b) Indirect soldering unit (with retardant, currently not available in Germany via the BOS, but available from the EU via the Community procedure or rescEU, or gel forming) c) Water transport (only with helicopters!) to a container in an area which is difficult to reach, for example, and from there with small pumps and conventional soldering unit via D and C hoses.

- to a container in an area which is difficult to reach, for example, and from there to be removed with small pumps and conventional soldering device via D and C hoses. The instructions for the safe soldering device from point 2.3.2 apply!
