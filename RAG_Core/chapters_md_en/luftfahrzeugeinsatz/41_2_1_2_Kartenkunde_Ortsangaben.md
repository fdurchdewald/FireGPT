# 2.1.2 Kartenkunde/Ortsangaben

- Today, the pilots work continuously with GPS coordinates based on the World Geodetic System 1984 - WGS 84. Page 10 In order to avoid errors, only WGS 84 coordinates are to be used in communication with the air traffic controllers.

- Page 10 In order to avoid errors, only WGS-84 coordinates should be used in communication with air traffic. Other coordinates such as UTM are to be converted beforehand.

- Other coordinates such as UTM can be converted beforehand. Example UTM: 32U PD 02529 17187 Correct: GPS: N 52° 29,709 E 010° 30,615 Incorrect: N 52,49517 E 10,51025 ("Google Format") If other coordinate formats are used, all aircraft involved in this operation must be able to use them to avoid misunderstandings.
