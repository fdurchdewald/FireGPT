# 3.

- The preparation and regular updating of a general civil protection plan, which must contain at least: (a) the alarm and reporting regulations; (b) the units, facilities and operational resources available for civil protection; (c) provisions on information and communication technology in the event of major damage and in the event of a disaster; (d) arrangements for cooperation with the services of the Bundeswehr. Where necessary, special alarm and operational plans as well as external emergency plans shall be drawn up and continued.

- Where necessary, special alarm and deployment plans as well as external emergency plans shall be drawn up and continued. The plans shall be coordinated with special alarm and deployment plans in the areas of health and the environment.
