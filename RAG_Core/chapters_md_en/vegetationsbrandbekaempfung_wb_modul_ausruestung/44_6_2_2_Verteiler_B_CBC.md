# 6.2.2 Verteiler B-CBC

- The distributor serves to distribute the extinguishing water supplied by a "larger" hose line into three "smaller" lockable hose lines. In the fire service practice, in particular in case of danger moments or in case of an extinguishing water transport over long distances, unwanted pressure shocks cannot be completely excluded.

- In the fire brigade practice, in particular in the event of danger moments or in case of an extinguishing water transport over long distances, unwanted pressure shocks cannot be completely excluded. Therefore, the use of pressure limitation valves should not be omitted for safety reasons.

- Therefore, for safety reasons, the use of pressure limitation valves should not be dispensed with. In this way, an overload of the valves and hoses is prevented.
