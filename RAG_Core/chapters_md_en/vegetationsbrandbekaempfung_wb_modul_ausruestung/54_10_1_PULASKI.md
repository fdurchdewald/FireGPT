# 10.1 PULASKI

- The "PULASKI" is a combination of pick and axe with an 80 cm long handle. The splitter edge and cutting edge are sharpened like an axe.

- The splinter edge and cutting edge are sharpened like an axe. It can be used for the following purposes: - freeing of gluten nests, - creating a safety strip or wound strip (up to the mineral bottom), also for stony terrain with tufty grass - removing roots or branches, - removing bark and bark on burnt tree trunks - splitting and clearing of tree trunks and stumps As a disadvantage, the short stalk is to be seen, which means that the fireman always has to bend very deeply.
