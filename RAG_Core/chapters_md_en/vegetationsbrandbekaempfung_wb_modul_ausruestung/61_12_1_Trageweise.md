# 12.1 Trageweise

- When carrying the hand tools the following must be observed: The tool - carry closely on the body and with the handles parallel to the floor, - do not carry over the shoulder so that another person is not endangered as soon as the wearer turns around and so also the hand tool is swirled around, or the wearer cannot hurt himself when falling down - always carry the hand tool on steep slopes on the valley side of the slope, so that one does not fall on the hand tool when tripping. In this way one can fall against the slope and one does not hurt oneself thus by the tool
