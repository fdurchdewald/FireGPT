# 4.

- Personal protective equipment The ideal protective clothing for combating vegetation fire is a two-layer protective clothing for the working firefighters. However, as this is very difficult to implement for first-time workers, but an unnecessary physical load should be rented, the usual single-layer firefighter protective suit according to FwDV 1 or GUV-C 53, §12 (1), such as according to HuPF parts 2 and 3 or DIN EN 469, performance level 1 is sufficient for the firefighters.

- Since this is very difficult for first-time personnel to implement, but an unnecessary physical load is to be rented, it is sufficient for the extinguishing attack as protective clothing of the usual single-layer firefighter protective suit according to FwDV 1 or GUV-C 53, §12 (1), as e.g. according to HuPF parts 2 and 3 or DIN EN 469, performance level 1. As personal protective equipment for a extinguishing device the following equipment parts are additionally retained for a extinguishing device: • 20 pieces of protective eyewear (tight closing, fog-free) • 20 pieces of vegetation fire mask with filter
