# 7.3.1 Einsatz von Handwerkzeugen

- The standard loading of fire trucks that comply with a standard includes a variety of hand tools that can be used in the fight against vegetation fires. These include tools for digging, shoveling and throwing sand and soil, but also for hacking, cutting and crushing as well as for extinguishing.
