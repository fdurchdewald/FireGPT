# 7.5

- As with all fires of fire class A, water is also suitable as a extinguishing agent for vegetarian fires. Since a large amount of water is usually required for these applications, certain preparations should be made during the deployment planning: z Exploring extinguishing water sampling points, which can be used even in cases of great drought z Easy accessibility and removal possibilities check z Plan water transport over long hose distances (hose length, height differences, location of pumps) z Set handling points z Further information on the fire brigade learning facility.
