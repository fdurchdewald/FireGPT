# 1. Abschnitt

- Tasks of the civil protection authorities § 1 Civil protection (1) The civil protection authorities have the task of preparing the fight against disasters, combating disasters and contributing to the temporary elimination of disaster damage (catastrophe protection). They have to take the measures that appear to be necessary according to their discretion.

- (2) Disaster within the meaning of this law is an occurrence, the life or health of many people or animals, the environment, significant material values or the vital supply of the population to such an unusual extent that it is necessary to place under the uniform management of the Civil Protection Authority a cooperation necessary for the defence and the fight against it between authorities, bodies and organisations.
