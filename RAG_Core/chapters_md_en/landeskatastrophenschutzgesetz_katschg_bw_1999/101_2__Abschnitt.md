# 2. Abschnitt

- Organization of civil protection authorities § 4 Civil protection authorities (1) Lower civil protection authorities are the municipal council offices and the mayoral offices of the municipal districts as lower administrative authorities. - Page 6 of 25 - (2) Higher civil protection authorities are the government offices.

- - Page 6 of 25 - (2) Higher civil protection authorities are the government presidencies. (3) Supreme civil protection authority is the Ministry of the Interior.

- (3) The Supreme Civil Protection Authority is the Ministry of the Interior. § 5 Civil Protection authorities, bodies, agencies and professional bodies (1) All civil protection authorities, institutions and bodies of the country, as well as legal persons under public law, which are subject to the supervision of the State and have their own responsibilities in the district of the Civil Protection Authority, the public hospitals and their institutions, the institutions and bodies of the Retention Service, as well as the chambers in accordance with the law on professional law and the chambers of doctors, dentists, veterinarians, pharmacists, psychotherapists and the children's and juvenile psychotherapists of the country are involved in the protection of the catastrophes within the scope of their duties.

- § 5 Civil protection authorities, bodies, bodies and professional bodies (1) All authorities, institutions and bodies of the country which are affiliated to the civil protection authority, as well as legal persons under public law, which are under the supervision of the State and have their own responsibilities in the civil protection authority's district, the publicly funded acute hospitals and their institutions, the institutions and bodies of the rescue service, as well as the chambers under the law on professional law and the chambers of doctors, dentists, veterinarians, pharmacists, psychological psychotherapists as well as the children's and juvenile psychotherapists of the country are involved in the field of catastrophen protection within the scope of their duties. The integrated control centres are bodies within the meaning of this regulation.

- The Integrated Control Centres are bodies within the meaning of this provision. The Civil Protection Authority coordinates the work of civil protection contributors with the exception of the highest national authorities.

- The Civil Protection Authority shall coordinate the work of civil protection workers, with the exception of the highest national authorities.
