# 2. Abschnitt

- Civil protection helpers § 11 (1) Civil protection helpers are men and women who are engaged in civil protection. They commit themselves to the institution of civil protection assistance for participation in civil protection, insofar as their duty of participation does not already exist due to their membership of the carrier.

- They undertake to assist the civil protection institution in so far as their duty of participation does not already exist on the basis of their membership of the carrier. (2) Unless otherwise provided by law, the rights and obligations of the helpers are only with regard to the organisation to which they belong.

- 2. Unless otherwise provided by law, the rights and obligations of the helpers shall apply only to the organisation to which they belong. In so far as the regulations applicable to the voluntary members of the local fire brigades apply mutatis mutandis to helpers who do not belong to an organisation.

- In so far as the regulations applicable to the volunteers of the local fire brigades apply mutatis mutandis to helpers who do not belong to any organisation. - Page 12 of 25 - (3) Civil protection volunteers exempted from military service or civil service are obliged to participate in the official events and operations of the civil protection forces.
