# 1. Abschnitt

- Indemnifications § 32 (1) Insofar as measures on the basis of this law have expropriating effect, the Katastrophenschutzbehörde, in whose district the measure was taken, must be provided by the Katastrophenschutzbehörde at the request of an appropriate compensation in money. (2) In the amount of the compensation benefits, claims of the claimant against third parties, which are aimed at the compensation of the property disadvantage suffered or the services rendered, are transferred to the cost bearer (§ 33 para. 2).

- (2) In the amount of the compensation, claims of the person entitled to compensation against third parties, which are directed at the compensation of the property disadvantage suffered or the services rendered, are transferred to the cost bearer (§ 33 para. 2). The transition cannot be asserted to the detriment of the person entitled to compensation.
