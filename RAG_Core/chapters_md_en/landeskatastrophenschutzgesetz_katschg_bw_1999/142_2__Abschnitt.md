# 2. Abschnitt

- Costs § 33 Costs (1) The country procures vehicles, equipment and special equipment within the framework of an annual equipment programme and makes them available to the disaster relief agencies for the purposes of civil protection. The country shall bear the costs of special training measures in civil protection, in particular in the context of exercises which are included in an annual training programme to be continued.
