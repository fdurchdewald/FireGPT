- Supporters of the Civil Protection Service § 12 Legal relations of the helpers (1) Supporters of the Civil Protection Service are the members of the units and facilities of the Civil Protection Service. The helpers are obliged to participate in the service events and the operations of the units and facilities of the Civil Protection Service.

- The helpers are obliged to participate in the official events and operations of the units and facilities of the Civil Protection Service. (2) No remuneration is granted for the activity as helpers of the Civil Protection Service.

- (2) A remuneration for the activity as a civil protection service assistant is not granted. § 13 Continuing benefits; refunds (1) The helpers may not suffer any disadvantages in the employment or service relationship from the service in the civil protection service.

- § 13 Continuation of benefits; reimbursements (1) The helpers may not suffer from the service in the civil protection service any disadvantages in the employment or service relationship.
