# 9. Festlegungen zu treffen, um den Schutz kritischer Infrastruktur langfristig und nachhaltig

- § 6 State Advisory Council for Fire and Civil Protection (1) The Ministry responsible for Fire and Civil Protection appoints a State Advisory Council for Fire and Civil Protection, which advises fundamental questions of fire protection, assistance and civil protection and discusses suggestions for the implementation of this law.
