# 2. PRÄVENTION, VORBEREITUNG UND WALDBRANDVERHÜTUNG

- By accompanying the forest ecosystems to increase resilience in climate change, on the one hand, forest fires are to be prevented, on the other hand, countermeasures in the event of a fire are to be supported or made possible.

- The following are listed below: • Application of a proper forest management in accordance with § 5 LWaldG - Selection of tree species - Reduction of the risk of fire by insertion of deciduous trees (e.g. beech, oak) in conifers and creation of depressing or stopping structures - Forest care • E.g. Aesthesia, afforestation, mixed forest management and thus a reduced risk of a complete tree species loss • Forest protection using environmentally friendly methods i.e. Section 15 LWaldG (e.g. no decommissioning work, no fire-hazard operation) • Keeping of undergrowth in order to prevent the function of a "burning ladder" • In justified exceptional cases after a hazard analysis takes place the installation of wound-and-strip - Outside the vegetation period from 1 March to 1 March until
