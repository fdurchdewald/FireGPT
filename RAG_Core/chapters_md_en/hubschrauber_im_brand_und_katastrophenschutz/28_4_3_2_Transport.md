# 4.3.2 Transport

- Remote areas or areas which are difficult to reach due to topography, such as mountain huts, are often dependent on the transport of materials and personnel via helicopter in the anti-aircraft sector (Brandl, 2023). This includes the provision of equipment such as hose material or portable pumps, as well as the transport of sandbags or dyke construction material.

- In addition to providing equipment such as hose material or portable pumps, this also includes the transport of sandbags or dike construction material. However, the supply of food and drinking water to the population is thus also possible.
